# Refer Back To Me

## Instructions
Each time i give a prompt, go through it and get back to me with a response that includes the following:
what you have understood, how are you planing to work on it, and what are the expected outcomes. This will help us ensure that we are on the same page and that I can provide you with the most accurate and helpful responses.
